## Acceptance Criteria

1. Human-decision items show exact question, why AI cannot decide, options, evidence per option, and downstream impact.
2. AI does not choose human-decision outcomes.
3. Authorized user decisions can be applied deterministically.
4. UI shows counts for safe fixes, approval suggestions, human decisions, and unsupported items.
5. Exactly one primary CTA is visible for the active copilot state.
6. Deterministic acceptance covers safe repairs, proposal accept/reject, human decision, revalidation, export-ready convergence, stale proposal, repeated loop, max iteration, unsupported visible, and no real AI.
7. Final report is created at `docs/governance/phase4/AI_RESUME_COPILOT_TARGET_COMPLETION_REPORT.md`.
